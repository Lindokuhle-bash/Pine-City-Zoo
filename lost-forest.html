LOST FOREST

Step into the mysterious Lost Forest — a lush, green world filled with dense foliage, hidden trails, and the sounds of nature all around you. It's a place where animals roam freely and nature thrives untouched.

What to experience:

  • Shaded nature walks through dense jungle
  • Rare bird and primate sightings
  • Hidden waterfalls and observation decks
  • Peaceful picnic spots under towering trees

Tips:

  • Wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Keep noise to a minimum to spot more wildlife
  • Bring a water bottle — it gets humid!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest begins behind the Amphitheatre and stretches along the eastern edge of the park.
