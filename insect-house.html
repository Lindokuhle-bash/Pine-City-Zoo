INSECT HOUSE

The Insect House is home to an amazing variety of insects, including butterflies, beetles, ants, and more. Discover the fascinating world of these tiny creatures and learn about their important roles in nature.

Highlights:

  • Live butterfly garden
  • Colorful beetle exhibits
  • Interactive ant colony display
  • Educational talks and demonstrations

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Insect House is located at pen I07, next to the Butterfly Garden.
