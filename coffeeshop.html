COFFEE SHOP

Need a quick break? Our Coffee Shop is the perfect place to relax and recharge during your zoo adventure. Whether you're craving a fresh cappuccino, a warm muffin, or a cool iced latte, we’ve got you covered.

Menu highlights:

  • Freshly brewed coffee & teas
  • Sandwiches & wraps
  • Pastries, muffins & vegan treats
  • Ice cream & cold drinks

Opening hours: 08:00 – 17:00 daily

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Coffee Shop is located near the central fountain, right across from the giraffe enclosure.
