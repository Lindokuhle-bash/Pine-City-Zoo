MONKEY TRAIL

Take an adventure through the lively Monkey Trail, a winding path through the trees where you can observe different monkey species swinging, climbing, and playing in their natural habitat.

What you’ll see:

  • Squirrel monkeys leaping through the canopy
  • Capuchins solving puzzles and foraging for food
  • Colorful tamarins and howler monkeys in action
  • Educational signs and monkey trivia along the trail

Visitor tips:

  • Walk slowly and quietly for the best sightings
  • Do not feed the monkeys
  • Have your camera ready — they move fast!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail entrance is located next to the Insect House and loops around the eastern edge of the Lost Forest.
