LION

The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the most recognizable big cats and is often referred to as the "king of the jungle." Lions are native to parts of Africa and a small population exists in Indiaâ€™s Gir Forest.

Lions are unique among big cats because they live in social groups called prides, which consist of related females, their cubs, and a few males. Male lions are known for their majestic manes, which make them stand out in the animal kingdom.

They are powerful predators that hunt large prey such as zebras, wildebeest, and buffalo. Lions are apex predators, playing a key role in maintaining the health and balance of their ecosystems.
